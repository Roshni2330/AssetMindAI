# 🏭 AssetMind AI

### AI-Powered Industrial Knowledge Intelligence Platform

AssetMind AI is an Industrial Knowledge Intelligence Platform designed to transform fragmented industrial documents into actionable operational intelligence.

Built for the **ET AI Hackathon 2.0**, the platform enables engineers, maintenance teams, and operations managers to quickly access critical information hidden across maintenance reports, inspection records, SOPs, and operational documents.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Industrial organizations often struggle with:

* Fragmented maintenance records
* Disconnected inspection reports
* Scattered SOPs and operational documents
* Loss of expert knowledge
* Slow information retrieval
* Inefficient troubleshooting and decision-making

Engineers spend significant time searching for information instead of solving operational problems.

AssetMind AI addresses this challenge by creating a unified knowledge layer across industrial documents.

---

## 💡 Solution Overview

AssetMind AI ingests industrial documents and transforms them into structured, searchable intelligence.

The platform:

* Extracts critical operational entities
* Identifies equipment, issues, and personnel
* Generates knowledge graphs
* Enables intelligent document exploration
* Provides operational insights through a unified dashboard

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📄 Industrial Document Processing

* Upload industrial PDF documents
* Automatic text extraction
* Support for maintenance and inspection records

### 🔍 Entity Extraction Engine

Automatically identifies:

* Equipment
* Operational Issues
* Engineers
* Maintenance Information

### 🕸 Knowledge Graph Generation

Creates relationships between:

* Equipment
* Engineers
* Issues
* Maintenance Activities

This enables connected knowledge discovery across documents.

### 📊 Industrial Insights Dashboard

Provides:

* Equipment statistics
* Issue tracking
* Personnel visibility
* Operational intelligence summaries

### 🤖 Operations Copilot

Allows users to interact with document intelligence using natural language queries.
